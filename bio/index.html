Kira Goldsmith is an actor who integrates singing and dancing to tell stories. Growing up near New York City, she was fortunate to see lots of great theatre, which fueled her interest in performing. Favorite credits include Catherine in “Pippin”, Audrey in “Little Shop of Horrors”, and Viola in “Twelfth Night”.

Kira graduated from the prestigious two-year musical theatre conservatory program CAP21 and was cast in a mainstage production of “On The Town”, in which she played a variety of fun characters and enjoyed the challenge of an energetic piece. While at CAP21, she helped to develop “Where It All Began”, a new alien spoof musical. Through this process she discovered her niche is the dork that is not afraid to tease herself and creating original pieces is her favorite part of this beautiful business. She is very excited for whatever piece of theatre comes next, and also couldn’t think of how to not say this last line any less cheesy.
